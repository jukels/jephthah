Our Mission

Provide comprehensive Christian education in a supportive environment that promotes excellence in learning, self discipline and motivation in ensuring that students become disciples of Jesus Christ and advance the global community


Our Vision

Train up a child in the way he should go and when he is old, he will not depart from it. -Proverbs 22:6


Our Philosophy

All scripture is given by inspiration of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness. We believe that the Bible is the only inspired and infallible authoritative word of god who is the author of all TRUTH.


Our Four Pillars

  • Pillar 1: Wisdom
    Academic thinking
  • Pillar 2: In favour with God
    (Spiritual formation)
  • Pillar 3: In Stature
    (Skill & Physical development)
  • Pillar 4: In favour with men
    (Social & emotional life)
    Luke 2:52

We provide an atmosphere in the school that promotes spiritual, mental, social and physical development as indicated in Luke 2:52 (And Jesus increased in wisdom and stature, and in favour with God and man). We believe that the total education of each student necessitates active collaboration with parents to whom God has given the primary responsibility of educating the child. We train our students to develop in four core areas (Our 4 Pillars)
